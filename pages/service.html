Frequently Asked Questions

What dental treatments does Sans Dental Studio offer?

Sans Dental Studio provides a wide range of boutique dental treatments, including Cosmetic Dentistry, Ultrasonic Scaling, Laser Dentistry, Orthodontic Care, Root Canal Treatment, Dentures, Periodontal Care, Dental Implants, and Professional Teeth Whitening.

Does Sans Dental Studio provide pain-free root canals and laser dentistry?

Yes, we specialize in gentle, minimally invasive laser treatments that offer faster healing times and maximum comfort. Our advanced single-visit root canals are designed to relieve tooth pain and save your natural teeth with minimal discomfort.

Who is the dentist at Sans Dental Studio?

Sans Dental Studio is led by Dr. Hamsapriya R, a highly experienced general and cosmetic dentist with over 15 years of clinical expertise. Dr. Hamsapriya R is BDS qualified and specializes in crafting personalized, compassionate dental solutions.

What are the locations and timings of Sans Dental Studio?

Sans Dental Studio has clinics in Thoraipakkam and Kancheepuram (Chennai, India). Our clinic timings are Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. You can contact us at +91 9941055547 to schedule an appointment.

Why should I choose Sans Dental Studio for cosmetic dentistry?

Sans Dental Studio combines state-of-the-art diagnostic technology with a boutique, calm environment. Under the guidance of Dr. Hamsapriya R, we offer custom veneer design, aesthetic tooth reshaping, and professional teeth whitening to deliver healthy, natural-looking smiles.
